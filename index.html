Frequently Asked Questions

What is your standard turnaround time?
For standard batches (under 30 images), the turnaround time is usually 24 to 48 hours. For larger bulk volume projects, a clear delivery schedule will be discussed and determined beforehand to match your timeline.
In what file formats do you deliver the final images?
I can deliver files according to your needs. Most e-commerce clients request high-resolution JPEG, transparent PNG, or fully layered PSD/TIFF files for their operational flexibility.
Do you offer a free trial or test sample?
Yes, absolutely. I understand the importance of quality assurance. You can send 1 or 2 sample images as a test project so you can review my precision and speed before committing to a full commercial contract.
How do we handle large file transfers?
We can use safe cloud platform storage solutions such as Google Drive, Dropbox, or WeTransfer. You can simply share the link to your raw product shots, and I will upload the refined visuals back to the same structured folder.
