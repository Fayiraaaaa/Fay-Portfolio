Frequently Asked Questions

What is your standard turnaround time?
Turnaround time depends on the batch volume. For standard batches (under 30 images), the turnaround time is usually 24 to 48 hours. For larger bulk volume projects, a delivery schedule will be discussed and determined beforehand to meet your operational timeline.
What file formats will the final images be delivered in?
I can deliver files according to your needs. Most e-commerce clients request high-resolution JPEG, transparent PNG, or fully compressed PSD/TIFF files for their operational flexibility.
Do you offer a free trial or test sample?
Yes, absolutely. I understand the importance of quality assurance. You can send 1 or 2 images as a trial so you can review my expertise, precision, and speed before committing to a full commercial contract.
How do we handle large file transfers?
We can use secure cloud platform storage solutions such as Google Drive, Dropbox, or WeTransfer. You can simply share the link to your raw product shots, and I will upload the refined visuals back to the same structured folder.
